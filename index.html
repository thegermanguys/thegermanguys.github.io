Germany is a land of endless possibilities, where innovation meets tradition, and where quality of life reigns supreme. From its vibrant cities to its breathtaking landscapes, Germany offers something for everyone. Imagine immersing yourself in a rich culture, experiencing unparalleled education and career opportunities, and enjoying a robust social safety net. Germany is a place where you can truly thrive and reach your full potential. Come, discover the Germany that awaits you!

How long does the visa application process take?

The visa application process can take several weeks or even months, depending on the type of visa you are applying for and the workload of the German embassy or consulate in Kathmandu. It is important to start the process well in advance of your intended travel date.

Can I cover my own expenses as student ?

Yes. Thats what most people do. Come here with blocked account to cover your initial expenses. Later, you can cover everything from your part time work including your rent, groceries and travelling once a while. Things will be different if you plan to come on private university though.

How much money does it take in total

For student, it would be ( blocked account: 12000euro, processing expenses incl. visa fee, uni admission fee, insurance: 3000euro, travel: 700euro, first month expense: 1500euro, other: 1000 euro ).
For other, depends on your contract and your visa type.

Is language required?

In most of the case, Yes. However there are exception where it's not rquired. Example: You are coming for employment that speaks english, your au-pair speaks the language other than german, your contract providier agrees to bring you without language and many more. It depends on your situation, contract and visa type.

What are the situation of Au-pair/ Ausbuldung visa rate?

Visa rate is subjective concepts. Only thing that is certain is you will get visa if you are applying genuenly. Write your intention clearly, read the contract, have the language, and have a plan, attend interview well and you will get visa.
