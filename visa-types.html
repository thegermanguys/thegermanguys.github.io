Job seeker visa

This visa allows you to enter germany for the purpose of finding employment. You need to have good profile to demonsterate your qualifications and you are allowed stay for 6 months (which can later be extended upto two times after arriving in germany). You need to cover your cost of living until you find employment. You cannot work outside the field you applied visa for and you can later switch to studies or other permit if required.

Student Visa

This visa allowes you to study higher education in germany. You need to show your high school or diploma and must be aceepted by the university admission before applying for visa. Your grades matter and depending on the university you can go with either IELTS or Goethe german certification. If you want to study in public university, you will be first studying studentkolleg. You must provide finincial means to survive within the duration of your visa by a sponser or income of your family members or scholarship.

Au-pair visa

This visa allowes you to come to germany as Au-pair. You will help a family mainly with taking care of their children. You will get visa for 1year which can later be extended or switched to another permit. Depending on the family, you might need to have language certificate and no finincial documents is required however this have some age restriction. You will be surviving under your host family and also granted additional cost to cover personal expenses.

Ausbuldung Visa

Similar to studies, this visa allows you to complete ausbuldung in germany. You will be studying and in parellel working for the same place. You will have more in-field specialization and little bit of theory with it. You need to have language certification and you will be paid by your workplace within the course of your contract.

Skilled professional visa

Allows professional to come to germany with the purpose of employment. You need to have signed working contract with the company and the contract should requires you to be present in germany. Depending on company you may or may not need any language certification hoever you need to have some basis to prove your qualification. No financial means is required but you need to have certain gross salary paid by company
